Curry Sweet Potatoes

This is a recipe for my famous roasted curry sweet potatoes! These are a year-round staple in my house.

Ingredients

  • 4-6 sweet potatoes
  • 1/4 cup of Coconunt Oil
  • Curry powder of choice
  • Pepper
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 F.
  2. Cut sweet potatoes in 1 inch cubes.
  3. Toss the sweet potatoes in a bowl with the coconut oil.
  4. Once coated in oil, add the curry powder, pepper, cinnamon, and salt.
  5. Place sweet potatoes on a baking sheet and place in the oven for 15 minutes.
  6. Remove sweet potatoes and mix up on the baking sheet. Place back in oven for 10 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven and let cool.
