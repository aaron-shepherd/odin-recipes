Miso Kale

This is a recipe I made for Miso Kale!

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch of kale (dinosaur kale works best, but any will do)
  • Red Miso paste
  • Minced garlic
  • Red Pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Remove kale leaves from stems. Chop up and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, heat on a medium heat. While the pan heats up, pour some olive oil on the kale and massage your kale (trust me).
  3. In a small pot, mix in your water and red miso paste. Stir until it dissolves.
  4. Do a dry sautee on your kale for a minute, then add a little more olive oil.
  5. Add some of your miso to the kale, as well as the red pepper flakes and garlic.
  6. Cook for 5 more minutes.
  7. Let cool.
