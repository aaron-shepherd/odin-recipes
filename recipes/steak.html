Best Steak Recipe

This is a recipe for a good medium steak.

Ingredients

  • 6-8oz Steak (preferably a New York Strip or Tomahawk cut)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Rosemary

Steps

  1. Season your steak to your liking. Salt and pepper only.
  2. Fire up your Green Egg smoker. Bring to 275-300F.
  3. Once temperature is achieved, put stick of butter in skillet. Once melted, place rosemary in butter.
  4. Reduce.
  5. Place steak in butter and cook for 5 minutes on each side. Be sure to baste the steak as it cooks.
  6. Once cooked, in another skillet, pan sear the steak on both sides for 1-2 minutes.
  7. Cut and serve.
