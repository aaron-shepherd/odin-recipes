Best Steak Recipe

This is a recipe for a good medium steak.

Ingredients

  • 6-8oz Steak (preferably a New York Strip or Tomahawk cut)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Rosemary

Steps

  1. Start up your Green Egg. Set temperature between 275F and 350F.
  2. Once hot enough, place skillet on grill and let heat up. After 5 minutes, place butter on skillet and let melt.
  3. Place rosemary in melted butter and reduce.
  4. Season steak with salt and pepper on both sides. Place on skillet and begin basting the steak as it cooks. Cook each side for 2 minutes.
  5. Remove steak from skillet, and place on another skillet to pan sear each side for a minute.
  6. Serve with rosemary from reduction.
